About Projectile Motion:

Projectile motion is the motion of an object thrown or projected into the air, subject only to the acceleration of gravity. The object is called a projectile, and its path is called its trajectory.

Key factors affecting projectile motion:

  • Launch Angle: Determines the direction of initial velocity
  • Initial Velocity: The speed at which the projectile is launched
  • Gravity: Pulls the projectile downward (9.8 m/s² on Earth)
